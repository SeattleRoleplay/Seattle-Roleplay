Rules

Seattle Roleplay Rules & Guidelines for In-Game

(1.1) Realistic Avatar; unrealistic avatars are prohibited.

(1.2) Random Deathmatch; you cannot kill someone for no reason whatsoever.

(1.3) Metagaming; watching of any player(s) stream to find out where that player is.

(1.4) New Life Rule; you are not to respawn and go back to the previous scenario you were in.

(1.5) Cuff Rushing; you must say cuffs/-cuffs- when arresting someone. You must transport them yourself or have another player transport your suspect.

(1.6) Green Zones; A place where no scenes can take place. These are in effect at the Fire Department, Police Department, and Sheriff's Office, Hospital, and Civilian Spawns.

(1.7) Off-Brand Departments; such as Bail Bonds, National Park Service, FBI, CIA, or any other off-brand departments. You cannot run SWAT, HEAT, or any other division other than Patrol unless you're actually a part of that department's aforementioned specialized unit(s).

(1.8) Store Robberies; to perform any robbery at any location that allows you to hold a robbery, you must have Staff approval. Major robberies such as the Bank, Jewelry Stores, and ATMs you must have more than two players assisting you.

(1.9) Fail RP; you must roleplay in an orderly fashion. Roleplay as if you were in the real world.

(1.10) Mafias/Gangs; you may not roleplay in a gang or in a mafia. Gangbangers don't go around in crews robbing and shooting people up for no reason in the real world.

(1.11) Graphiti Tool; You are not allowed to randomly vandalize public roadways, buildings, sidewalks, etc. This tool is only to be used if it is part of your roleplay.

(1.12) Interfering with Scenarios; If a scenario is taking place, and you were never a part of that scenario to begin with, you are taking away from another player's roleplay time.

(1.13) Priority: Priority is required for hostage, kidnapping, armed robbery, etc⁠

Vehicle Rules & Guidelines

(2.1) Vehicle Colors; you must use realistic vehicle colors. (Bright Pink, Bright Green, Bright Yellow, etc.)

(2.2) GTA Driving / VDM / Realism; you are to drive realistic. You are not to drive in opposite lanes of oncoming traffic. You are to follow all traffic laws when you are roleplaying unless it is a part of your roleplay.

(2.3) Exotics; you are not to use any exotic vehicle unless you are a Server Booster, VIP, or Contributor. The Bugatti is banned for all players. It is not to be used.

(2.4) Excessive Honking; You are not allowed to spam your vehicle horn.

(2.5) All ATV and 4 wheeler's on the State and Police teams are for whitelisted players only!

Point System

Every player is allowed to get 16 points. Once you reach 16 or more points, your account will be permanently banned from the Discord server. Depending on the severity, you may be permitted to appeal it and be given another chance.

Points can be obtained by:

  • Temporary Ban = 4 points
  • Kick, Mute/Timeout, and = 2 points
  • Warning = 1 point

Guidelines Policy

By joining our community, you agree to follow our rules. Staff have the final say in any moderation action given and can remove you from this community at any time if they deem the actions, you are doing are egregious. These rules are generalized guidelines and are subject to change at any point in time.

Discord Rules & Guidelines

(1.1). Your Discord server nickname must be set with your ROBLOX username.

(1.2). No mentioning VIPs, Server Boosters or Staff Members.

(1.3). No NSFW, pornographic, or sexually explicit content. This includes screen sharing of very derogatory content. This will end up being a permanent ban.

(1.4). No illegal content, privacy, or hacking.

(1.5). No publishing of personal information (including real names, addresses, emails, passwords, bank accounts, credit card information, etc.).

(1.6). No personal attacks. This includes harassment, sexism, racism, hate speech, or offensive language.

(1.7). No religious discussions, political discussions, or sexual discussions. We do not need this in our community. You all are children.

(1.8). No flaming, flame wars, trolling, spamming, excessive messaging, or walls of text (either in separate posts or as a single post).

(1.9). No overusing emojis or overusing reactions. No NSFW external emojis.

(1.10). Keep conversations in English. Moderators reserve the right to delete any post if it violates our guidelines.

(1.11). No advertising at all. Any advertising will result in a permanent ban from both in-game and Discord.

(1.12). Stealing assets including Logos, Official Media Photos, Documentation, etc. is strictly prohibited.

Voice Channel Guidelines

(2.1). No Voice Channel hopping.

(2.2). No annoying, loud, or high-pitched noises.

(2.3). Reduce the amount of background noise.

(2.4). No voice changers or soundboards unless it's provided by this community.

(2.5). Use the proper voice channels while roleplaying. Civilians must be in Civilian VCs at all times while roleplaying unless required to go to a traffic stop or scenario. All Emergency services must be in the correct RTO.
